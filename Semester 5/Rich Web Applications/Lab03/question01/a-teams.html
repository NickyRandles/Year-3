Arsenal F.C

Arsenal Football Club are a Premier League football club based in Holloway, London. One of the most successful clubs in English football, they have won 13 First Division and Premier League titles and a joint record 11 FA Cups.

Aston Villa F.C

Aston Villa Football Club are an English professional football club based in Witton, Birmingham. Founded in 1874, they have played at their current home ground, Villa Park, since 1897. Aston Villa were founder members of The Football League in 1888.
