About Ratoath Community Centre

Ratoath Community Centre celebrates its 7th year this September. Over the past four years thousands of local residents, of all ages, have passed through our doors for all sorts of activities as well as visiting our Venue Theatre.

This community facility is your facility and we want you to use it to your advantage.

This year we have a fantastic range of activities in our Autumn Programme. You can keep fit, watch your weight, your children can avail of dance classes (Irish, hip-hop, ballet, jazz), learn acting skills, art for young and old, senior citizens, plus many more exciting and exhilarating activities for you and your family to choose from.

I hope you will keep this guide as a reference for our activities and that you will visit RCC over the coming months.
