Glee Club - sing dance act

Glee Club is now coming to Ratoath community centre!

Give your child the opportunity to sing, dance and act like theyve seen it done on shows like glee or fame.

Glee Club is for children aged between 4-12. The emphasis on our club is to nourish your childs inner talents for performance, while also helping them develop socially, personally and artistically all in a fun and safe environment. The art of performance can help boost your childs confidence while still having fun and meeting new friends.

Some of the reasons children attend glee club

To boost self confidence. Taekwondo Olympic Style for Kids

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art in which we teach our students how to kick, punch, block, defend themselves, build their confidence, develop their skills and encourage them to be respectful people. Respect begins with complete respect for oneself. If you respect your body and yourself, you are more likely to respect others. All this is done is a safe and friendly environment.

Ratoath Olympic Taekwondo is under the instruction of 5th Dan Black Belt Master Joe Doyle, one of Ireland most respected Taekwondo Masters.

Master Joe has over the past year, invited World Champions and Grand Masters to Ireland to conduct seminar for his student.

Classes are ongoing and you can join at any time. Ratoath Olympic Taekwondo train in the Ratoath Community Centre on TUESDAYs Only

4 - 11 year olds : TUESDAY : 4.00pm

12 - 16 year olds : TUESDAY : 5.05pm

17 years and ADULTS: TUESDAY : 7.15pm

This is a friendly class environment in which we cater for all ages and abilities.

The Academy of Russian Ballet

Monica Loughman, Irelands most famous ballerina and artistic director of the Irish Youth Russian Ballet, is excited to be returning to Ratoath Community Centre.

Monica has a truly unique approach to teaching ballet. Inspiring the children with demonstrations and encouraging them to develop their individual talents in a warm and friendly environment. After travelling and performing all over the world, Monica feels it?s time to invest in Irish children and bring back her expertise and knowledge to Ireland... Monica s goal is to bring ballet of an International standard to Ireland.

Junior classes are taught by Ms Aine McNamara under the direct supervision of Ms Loughman. All classes have a fun and friendly atmosphere that are designed to bring out the best in every child. Advanced booking would be advised. For more information go to www.irishballerina.com
